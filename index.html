About Me

Hello!

I am a User Experience Designer pursuing my love of design and form.

Earlier, in my 5 years as an Architect I utilized my design skills focussing on improving the energy efficiency of buildings. I enjoy problem solving and approach design problems with the attitude of understanding the root cause of any issue. In taking a holistic approach to design I dive deep, analyze the data and use it to effect the right solution.

Why UX?

User Experience Design has been a natural evolution and coming together of my skills, experience and interest. It aligns with my design aptitude and closely connects me with people using my design solutions. It also opens new prospects of learning how to integrate the ever evolving technology into people's lives.

Approach

Because no two projects are the same, I adapt my strategy and process with every new challenge. Summarized below are the basic fundamentals I follow in my design process:

Research | Listen, Observe and Learn

While convenient to work on a solution of a problem as it is presented, I make it a point to conduct due diligence, ask right questions to right people, and let data drive the solution.

Analyze | Uncover and Understand

It is easy to carry bias when analyzing data. Over the years, discipline & an open mind have helped me understand and interpret data correctly, leading to reinforcing or reworking the goals.
