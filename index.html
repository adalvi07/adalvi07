An Architect turned User Experience Designer, I believe in creating effective, reliable and delightful solutions. I seek opportunities that challenge me to think outside the box and convert those ideas into reality.

My Journey into User Experience Design

I have a Masters in Architecture and 5 years of Design experience. I have worked on some really good design projects in my career and while that gave me great joy, I felt a disconnect with the end-user of my product. When designing I always had their best interest in mind but I felt it would be a different experience getting to know how they actually feel, what their real needs, desires or frustrations are.

User Experience Design not only resonates with my core beliefs and design thinking but also closely connects me with people using my design solutions. It also opens new prospects of learning how to integrate the ever evolving technology into people's lives.
